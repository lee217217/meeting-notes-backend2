Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 30, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Meeting Workspace ("we", "our", "us") handles your information when you use our service at meeting-workspace (the "Service").

1. What we collect

  • Meeting content you paste or upload. Text, notes, or transcript files you submit to generate a summary.
  • Technical data. IP address, browser type, and basic request metadata (used only to prevent abuse).
  • Payment data. If you upgrade to Pro, payment is processed by Lemon Squeezy. We do not store your card details.
  • Local device data. Your quota count, Pro license key, language preference, and up to 20 recent meetings are stored in your browser's localStorage — not on our servers.

2. How we use it

  • To generate meeting summaries, action items, and follow-up emails.
  • To enforce daily free-tier limits and validate Pro licenses.
  • To improve reliability, debug errors, and prevent abuse.

3. How your meeting content is processed

When you run the workflow, your meeting notes are sent to Perplexity Sonar API (our AI provider) to generate the output. The content is:

  • Processed in-memory by our Netlify serverless function.
  • Not stored on our servers or database.
  • Discarded immediately after the response is returned to your browser.
  • Subject to Perplexity's own data handling policy for AI inference.

Your local history (last 20 meetings) lives only in your browser and can be cleared anytime from the history panel.

4. Third-party services we use

  • Perplexity AI — AI content generation. See Perplexity Privacy Policy.
  • Lemon Squeezy — Payment processing & license management. See Lemon Squeezy Privacy Policy.
  • Netlify — Hosting & serverless functions. See Netlify Privacy Policy.

5. Cookies

We do not use tracking cookies. We only use localStorage for app preferences (language, theme, quota, Pro license, and local history).

6. Data retention

Meeting content: not retained. Payment records: retained by Lemon Squeezy per their policy (typically 7 years for tax compliance). Local device data: retained until you clear your browser storage.

7. Your rights

Because we do not store your meeting content, there is nothing on our side to export or delete. For payment records, contact Lemon Squeezy directly. You may clear all local app data by clearing your browser's site data for this domain.

8. Children

The Service is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Security

All traffic is encrypted in transit via HTTPS. API keys and license secrets are stored as Netlify environment variables, never exposed to the browser.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.

11. Contact

Questions about this policy? Email us at support@meeting-workspace.app.
