Privacy Policy

Last updated: 7 May 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how AI Meeting Notes (the "Service", "we", "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our website and services.

1. Who we are

AI Meeting Notes is operated by [OWNER_NAME], an independent software developer based in Hong Kong SAR. For privacy-related inquiries, contact: support@aimeetingnotes.com.

2. Information we collect

  • Meeting notes you input: text you paste or type into the app. Processed in real-time and not stored on our servers beyond the processing window.
  • Email address: optional — only if you sign up for the Starter tier or purchase a Pro/Max subscription.
  • License key & device identifier: stored locally in your browser (localStorage) and sent to our server for license verification and device-binding enforcement.
  • Usage quotas: run counters stored locally and mirrored on our server to enforce plan limits.
  • Analytics: anonymised page views and button events via Umami (a privacy-friendly, cookie-free analytics tool). No IP addresses or personal identifiers are stored.
  • Payment data: processed entirely by Lemon Squeezy. We do NOT see or store your credit card, bank, or billing address.

3. How we use information

  • To provide the AI summarisation service.
  • To enforce subscription limits and prevent abuse.
  • To communicate service-related updates (only if you subscribed).
  • To improve the product via aggregated, anonymised analytics.

4. How we protect data

  • All traffic uses HTTPS/TLS encryption.
  • Meeting notes are NOT written to any persistent database — they are sent to the AI provider for processing, then discarded.
  • Access to production infrastructure is restricted and MFA-protected.

5. Third-party services

  • AI processing: Perplexity AI / OpenAI (depending on plan). Governed by their respective privacy policies.
  • Payments: Lemon Squeezy (Merchant of Record). See their privacy policy.
  • Hosting: Netlify.
  • Analytics: Umami (self-hosted / EU region, no cookies, no personal data).

6. Cookies

We do NOT use tracking cookies. We only use browser localStorage to remember your language, theme, usage quota, and license key.

7. Data retention

  • Meeting notes: never stored server-side.
  • Email / license key: kept for as long as your subscription is active, plus 12 months for tax/accounting purposes, then deleted.
  • Analytics: aggregated, retained up to 24 months.

8. Your rights (GDPR / CCPA)

You have the right to access, correct, delete, or port your personal data. To exercise these rights, email support@aimeetingnotes.com. We respond within 30 days.

9. Children

The Service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

10. Changes

We may update this Policy from time to time. Material changes will be notified via email (to subscribed users) and/or a banner on the site.

11. Contact

Questions? Email support@aimeetingnotes.com.
