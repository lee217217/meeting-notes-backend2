This Privacy Policy explains how {{COMPANY_NAME}} ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information in connection with the Meeting Workspace service available at {{WEBSITE_URL}} (the "Service"). By using the Service, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum data required to operate the Service.

  • Meeting content you provide. Notes, transcripts, meeting titles, and settings you enter are transmitted to third-party AI providers for processing. We do not store this content on our servers after your request completes.
  • Technical data. IP address, browser type, device information, and timestamps of requests, used for rate limiting, abuse prevention, and service reliability.
  • Payment data. If you purchase a paid plan, billing information is collected and stored by our payment processor (Stripe). We only receive limited metadata such as your email, subscription status, and last 4 digits of your card.
  • Optional contact data. If you email us, we keep the message to respond to you.

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide and operate the Service (generate summaries, action items, follow-up emails).
  • To enforce usage limits and prevent abuse.
  • To process payments and manage subscriptions.
  • To respond to customer support requests.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

3. Data Retention

  • Meeting content: Not stored. Processed in memory and discarded once the response is returned.
  • IP-based quota records: Up to 24 hours, then automatically deleted.
  • Payment records: Retained by Stripe according to their policy and applicable law (generally 7 years).
  • Support emails: Retained for up to 24 months unless you request deletion earlier.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following sub-processors to deliver the Service:

  • Perplexity AI — generates meeting outputs based on your notes.
  • Netlify — hosts our website and serverless functions.
  • Stripe — processes payments and manages subscriptions.

Each third party processes data under their own privacy policy. By using the Service you consent to this sharing.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data, and to object to or restrict certain processing. To exercise any right, email us at {{CONTACT_EMAIL}}.

6. Cookies & Local Storage

We use browser local storage to remember your preferences (language, theme) and, if applicable, your Pro license key. We do not use tracking cookies for advertising.

7. Security

Data in transit is encrypted with HTTPS. We do not retain meeting content after processing. However, no system is 100% secure. You acknowledge this when you use the Service.

8. Children

The Service is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe we have, contact us and we will delete it.

9. International Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries other than your own. By using the Service you consent to such transfers.

10. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Material changes will be announced on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance.

1. 我們蒐集的資料

我們僅蒐集營運本服務所必要的最少資料。

  • 您提供的會議內容。您輸入的筆記、逐字稿、會議主題與設定會傳送至第三方 AI 服務處理。請求完成後，我們不會在伺服器上留存這些內容。
  • 技術性資料。IP 位址、瀏覽器類型、裝置資訊與請求時間戳，用於速率限制、防止濫用與服務穩定性。
  • 付款資料。若您購買付費方案，帳單資訊由我們的付款服務商（Stripe）蒐集與儲存。我們僅取得有限的後設資料，例如您的 email、訂閱狀態、信用卡末四碼。
  • 選擇性聯絡資料。若您寄 email 給我們，我們會保留該訊息以回覆您。

2. 我們如何使用資料

  • 提供與營運本服務（產生摘要、行動項目、跟進 Email）。
  • 執行使用量限制、防止濫用。
  • 處理付款與訂閱管理。
  • 回應客戶支援需求。
  • 遵守法律義務。

3. 資料保存期限

  • 會議內容：不儲存。在記憶體中處理，回應後立即丟棄。
  • IP 配額紀錄：最多保存 24 小時後自動刪除。
  • 付款紀錄：由 Stripe 依其政策與適用法律保存（通常為 7 年）。
  • 客服 email：最多保存 24 個月，您亦可要求提早刪除。

每一個第三方皆依其自身的隱私政策處理資料。使用本服務即表示您同意上述資料分享。
