Frequently asked questions

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancel anytime via your Lemon Squeezy customer portal. You keep access until the end of the current billing period.

What's your refund policy?

14-day money-back guarantee on your initial subscription. Email support@meetingworkspace.app within 14 days and we'll refund, no questions asked.

What happens if I hit my quota?

You'll see a friendly upgrade prompt. Your usage resets automatically — daily for Free/Starter, weekly for Pro, monthly for Max.

Can I use one license on multiple devices?

Yes. Pro supports 2 active devices, Max supports 5. You can deactivate and move between devices anytime.

Is my meeting data stored?

No. Meeting notes are processed in real-time and never written to our database. Only your email (if registered) and license key are stored. See our Privacy Policy.

Which AI model is used?

Best-in-class models (Perplexity / OpenAI) selected per task through our 5-agent orchestration: Coordinator → Summarizer → Action Item Agent → Email Agent → QA Review.

Do you offer team or enterprise plans?

Not yet — individual plans first. Need 10+ seats? Email support@meetingworkspace.app and we'll figure something out.

What languages are supported?

Input: any language. Output: optimised for English and Traditional Chinese (繁體中文). More coming soon.
