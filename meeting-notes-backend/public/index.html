Frequently asked

Where are my meeting notes stored?

Nowhere on our server. Each request is processed in memory and discarded once the response returns. The notes are sent to Perplexity's API for generation only.

Which languages are supported?

The AI output supports English, Traditional Chinese, Simplified Chinese and Japanese. The UI currently supports English and Traditional Chinese.

Can I upload Zoom or Teams transcripts?

Yes, drag-and-drop .vtt or .txt transcript files on the notes area. The content will be extracted and pasted automatically.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Pro is billed monthly and can be canceled anytime. You keep access until the end of your billing period.
